Return Policy

Epsilon-delta Inc. is committed to providing fair return policies to ensure user satisfaction with the Quilla service.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • For digital service subscriptions, full refunds are available if requested within 7 days from the service start date.
  • Refund requests must include a clear reason, and services already utilized may be excluded from the refund.

How to Request a Refund

Refunds can be requested via email at contact@epsilondelta.ai. Please include your order number and detailed reasoning in your request.

Processing Time

Refund requests will be processed within 7 business days upon receipt. Additional time may be required for the refund to reflect, depending on the payment method.

Exceptions

  • Items purchased with promotional codes are non-refundable.
  • Excessive refund requests may result in restrictions on service usage.
